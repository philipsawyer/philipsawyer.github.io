Upwake

Upwake is a new kind of alarm clock for your phone. It wakes you gently with the weather, news headlines, daily calendar events, reminders, and emails. Ease out of bed the easy way as you listen to what the new day will bring.

Fusion.

Fusion is a new iPhone app that let's you combine and view your Facebook, Twitter and Instagram accounts simultaneously! No need to go back and forth between social networks. With this app, it's all in one easy location.

Attendance!

Attendance is a simple but powerful way to stay on top of who's present and who's been skipping out. A perfect companion for anything from sports practice to calculus class.

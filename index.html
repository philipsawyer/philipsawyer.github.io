About Me

I have always had an interest in technology, but it wasn't until Apple released the iOS SDK back in 2008 that my interest really took off. I spent the next few years reading books and working through online tutorials in my spare time learning how to code and put together an iOS app.
At the very beginning of 2013, I lauched my first iPhone app called 'Attendance!' It was a simple way for teachers to take attendance in their classrooms. Later the same year I launched 'Fusion.' It combined all of the major social networks so you can look at them at the same time.
Fast forward to today, I am currently a sophomore studying Software Engineering at Auburn Univerity (War Eagle!). I now have experience in HTML, Java, PHP, and Swift programming. I have several apps I am currently working on which should be released soon.

Upwake Privacy Policy

By using Upwake you are accepting the policy defined below.

Your data is never stored on our servers and we cannot view any data you let the app use.

Your location is only used when you allow it, and it is only accessed when the alarm goes off and you have selected the weather as one of the options. It will then collect you location and send it to OpenWeatherMap which will then send Upwake the weather for your location.

Your Calendar is only accessed when you allow it, and is only access when the alarm goes off and you have selected Calendar as one of the options.

Your Reminders are only accessed when you allow it, and is only access when the alarm goes off and you have selected Reminders as one of the options.

Your Email is only accessed when you allow it, and is only access when the alarm goes off and you have selected Email as one of the options. Your credentials are stored locally on your device and are never visible to anyone else.
